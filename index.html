Diet Doctor Family

DDF is app deals with Diet Doctor and Family that will
provide a platform to a family to consult with nutritionist doctor about the his health condition and diet recomendations.

Diet Portal

Doctor can suggest different diet plan for range of age group within family

Doctors Portal

Subscribe one or two nutritionist doctors with the aim maximum avoidance of medicine and prioritize diet

Family Portal

Family members above 18 will use app to consult with doctors, while under 18 will use family head or other member.
